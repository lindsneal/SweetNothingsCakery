About Us

Mission

Bringing joy to people through cake.

Sweet Nothings Cakes and Confections was established in 2009. My name is Lindsay Neal, I am the chief cake artist and owner of Sweet Nothings. Baking is a hobby that is close to my heart. I love to design and create unique, one of a kind cakes based on the many things people love and embody. The most rewarding part of the caking process is the customer's reaction when they see the finished cake. Although Sweet Nothings is known for speciality cakes, we also make an assortment of delightful cupcakes, cookies and other treats to satisfy customer desires.

From birthdays to anniversaries, all occasions deserve a special cake that symbolizes the importance of the event. Any idea that comes to your mind can be turned into a creative and original cake, so let's come up with a cake unlike anything else.

Please look through the photo gallery and tell us what you like.

I would love to make your cake dreams come true!
